HES is an organization focused on achieving health equity in Connecticut.

Connecticut has a long way to go towards achieving health equity, according to the America’s Health Rankings report of 2019. Health disparities still plague our state.

The collection of race, ethnicity and language (REL) data is a critical component of evaluating health outcomes among various populations and ensuring health equity for everyone.

Community health workers improve health care in individuals and communities outside of the clinical setting.
